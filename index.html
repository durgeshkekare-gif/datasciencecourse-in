Decision guide

Which data science program should you choose?

Want hands-on + campus immersion
→ IIT Roorkee Applied DS & AI — optional campus modules at Roorkee. Most practical curriculum with real-world datasets.
Want GenAI and LLM specialisation
→ IIT Madras ML, GenAI & LLMs — NIRF #1 institution, specifically built for Generative AI use cases.
Want business + analytics (non-tech)
→ IIM Kozhikode Analytics & GenAI — management approach to analytics, IIM credential, best for business professionals.
Want global recognition
→ IIT Madras + Rotman UofT — joint India-Canada credential at ₹1.61L, combining NIRF #1 IIT with QS-ranked Canadian B-School.

FAQ

It depends on the program. IIT Roorkee's Applied DS & AI is designed for both technical and non-technical professionals — basic familiarity with mathematics is helpful but not mandatory. IIT Madras GenAI program requires a STEM background. IIM Kozhikode Analytics is specifically designed for non-technical business professionals with no coding prerequisite.
IIT programs focus on technical depth — Python, ML algorithms, deep learning, model building and implementation. They are best for professionals who want hands-on technical skills. IIM Kozhikode's Analytics & GenAI program takes a business strategy approach — how to use analytics and AI for business decisions, without requiring you to write code. Choose IIT for technical roles; choose IIM for management and business roles.
All programs are designed for working professionals and run on weekends. Live sessions are typically on Saturdays or Sundays. Recordings are available for replay. You do not need to take leave from work for any of these programs.
