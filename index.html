Decision guide

Which program type is right for you?

Working professional, want fast upskill
→ IIT/IIM Certificate (6–12 months). Weekends only. No career break. ₹1.5–4L. IIT/IIM credential for resume.
Want full degree + working
→ BITS Pilani WILP M.Tech or IIT Madras Online B.S. 2–4 years, part-time. Full degree credential. ₹2–6L.
Business/management professional, no coding
→ IIM Kozhikode Applied Analytics. 1 year. No coding required. Business-focused. IIM credential.
Senior leader, targeting CDO/CTO role
→ IIT Kanpur CDAO or IIT Bombay CTO Programme. 9–12 months. Executive-level credential. AI strategy focus.
Fresher / recent graduate
→ IIT Madras Online B.S. or BITS Pilani B.Sc DS & AI. Full degree. Start career in DS without JEE.
Online M.Sc at affordable fee
→ Manipal MUJ, LPU, Chandigarh University, Amity. ₹1.5–3L. 2-year online M.Sc. UGC-DEB approved.

FAQ

An IIT certificate (6–12 months, ₹1.5–4L) is a professional credential — not a degree. It signals expertise to employers in the private sector and is ideal for fast career upskilling. A BITS Pilani M.Tech (2 years, ₹4–6L total) is a full postgraduate degree awarded by an Institution of Eminence — equivalent to any M.Tech for academic and government purposes. Choose the certificate for speed and specific skill-building; choose the M.Tech if you want a degree credential that qualifies for PhD programs or government grade requirements.
