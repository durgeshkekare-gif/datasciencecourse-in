IIT Roorkee Applied Data Science & AI Programme 2026 — Complete Review

What is the IIT Roorkee Applied DS & AI programme?

The Post Graduate Certificate Programme in Applied Data Science & AI from IIT Roorkee's Continuing Education Centre (CEC) is a ~6-month online program for working professionals. Total fee: ₹1.5L + GST (₹1,500 application fee + ₹1,48,500 programme fee, payable in 2 equal instalments).

Curriculum

The curriculum covers: data exploration and preprocessing, machine learning (supervised and unsupervised), deep learning, Generative AI and GPTs, time series analysis, NLP, computer vision, real-world project work. 75+ lecture hours + 60+ practical hours. Two optional campus immersion modules at IIT Roorkee's Greater Noida Extension Centre.

Who should apply?

IT and software professionals wanting to transition into data science roles. Business analysts wanting to add ML skills. Engineers looking for an IIT credential in AI/DS. The program suits both technical and semi-technical professionals — basic Python familiarity is helpful but the program teaches Python from scratch.

Is ₹1.5L worth it?

For the IIT Roorkee credential + structured curriculum + hands-on projects + optional campus immersion — yes, for professionals who will actively use the credential for career advancement. Compared to private bootcamps charging ₹2–4L for similar content without institutional brand, IIT Roorkee at ₹1.5L is competitive value.
