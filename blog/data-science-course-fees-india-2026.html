Data Science Course Fees India 2026 — From Free to ₹6L Compared

Data science course fee ranges in India 2026

Data science education spans an enormous fee range in India — from free (NPTEL, DeepLearning.AI audit) to ₹6L+ (BITS Pilani M.Tech). Understanding what you get at each price point is essential before committing.

Free — NPTEL, DeepLearning.AI, fast.ai

NPTEL from IIT consortium: 200+ DS and ML courses, free to learn, ₹1,000–3,000 certificate exam. DeepLearning.AI on Coursera: free to audit. Best for building skills, not credential signalling.

₹1.5L–2L — IIT certificates (best value)

IIT Madras GenAI & LLMs (~₹1.5L), IIT Roorkee Applied DS & AI (~₹1.5L), IIT Guwahati DS & ML (~₹1.5L). Best combination of institutional brand, technical curriculum and fee efficiency. 6 months, weekend, continue working. Compare all at datasciencecourse.in/compare.

₹2L–4L — IIM analytics certificates and ₹3–6L full degrees

IIM certificates (no coding, business analytics focus), BITS Pilani M.Tech (full degree, 2 years). The right choice depends on whether you want speed and credential (IIT certificate), business analytics brand (IIM), or a full degree (BITS).
