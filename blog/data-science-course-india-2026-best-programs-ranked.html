Data science course vs data science degree — which to choose?

Choose a certificate (IIT, IIM, 6 months) if: you already have a degree (BCA/B.Tech/MBA) and just need to add data science skills + IIT credential to your CV. You're working and can't take 2–4 years off. You want the fastest ROI — 6 months study, then apply for DS roles immediately.

Choose a degree (IIT Madras BSc DS, BITS MTech) if: you're a student or recent graduate without an engineering background, you want depth (not just a credential), or you're targeting research/PhD path. The IIT Madras BSc DS is the best degree option — flexible exit after Year 1/2/3 means you can stop at certificate/diploma if the full degree isn't needed.

Free vs paid data science courses India — honest verdict

Free options (Coursera Andrew Ng, NPTEL, Kaggle Learn) are excellent for learning concepts and building skills. They are NOT sufficient for career transitions where employers need a credential to shortlist you. The IIT certificate (~₹1.5L) solves the credential problem — the 'IIT' name gets you through ATS/HR screening that free courses won't. Best approach: use free resources to learn, then get one IIT/IIM certificate for the credential. Don't pay ₹3–5L for a private ed-tech course when you can get an IIT cert for ₹1.5L.
