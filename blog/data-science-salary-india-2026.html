Data Science Salary in India 2026 — What to Realistically Expect

Data science salaries in India 2026 — the honest picture

Data science salaries in India vary enormously based on experience level, industry, city, company size and specific skills. The "average data scientist earns ₹8–10 LPA" figure circulated online is misleading because it mixes freshers and senior professionals. A more useful breakdown by experience level:

Experience levelTypical roleSalary range (India)
0–2 yearsJunior DS / Data Analyst₹4–8 LPA
2–5 yearsData Scientist / ML Engineer₹8–18 LPA
5–10 yearsSenior DS / Lead Scientist₹18–35 LPA
10+ yearsPrincipal / Head of DS / CDO track₹35L–1Cr+

Which sectors pay the most for data science in India

BFSI (Banking, Financial Services, Insurance) — highest paying for DS in India. Fraud detection, credit scoring, risk modelling and algorithmic trading command premium salaries. Senior DS in BFSI: ₹25–50 LPA in major banks and fintech firms.

Technology product companies — strong pay especially at unicorns and multinationals (Amazon, Google, Microsoft, Flipkart, Swiggy, Zomato). Compensation often includes ESOP which significantly raises total comp.

Consulting firms — McKinsey, BCG, Deloitte, Accenture are building large DS teams. Entry from MBA backgrounds with analytics skills typically starts ₹12–18 LPA.

Manufacturing and traditional sectors — lower but growing. DS roles in pharma, automotive, FMCG and industrial companies typically pay ₹8–20 LPA for 3–8 years exp.

How IIT/IIM certification affects salary

For career switchers moving into data science, an IIT or IIM certificate can justify a 20–40% salary premium over equivalent candidates without institutional credentials. The credential signals structured learning, analytical rigour and commitment to the field. More importantly, it helps clear initial HR screening — many DS job descriptions at large companies explicitly filter for educational credentials from recognised institutions.

What matters more than the certificate for senior salary

After the first 3–5 years, actual portfolio of work matters far more than certificates. The professionals earning ₹30L+ in data science in India are those who can demonstrate business impact — revenue generated, cost saved, systems built that scale. Certificate helps you get in the door; the work you do after determines how far you go.
