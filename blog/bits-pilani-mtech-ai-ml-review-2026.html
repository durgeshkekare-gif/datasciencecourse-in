BITS Pilani M.Tech AI & ML (WILP) 2026 — Review, Fee & Is It Worth It?

BITS Pilani M.Tech AI & ML — what makes it different

BITS Pilani's WILP M.Tech in AI & ML is the only M.Tech from a premier Indian institution requiring no GATE, allowing you to continue working full-time and completed in 2 years. BITS Pilani is an Institution of National Importance — same status as IITs.

Fee, structure and curriculum

Total fee: ~₹3–6L over 2 years. No GATE required. Weekend live sessions, 2 campus visits. Curriculum: ML fundamentals, deep learning, NLP, computer vision, reinforcement learning, MLOps, AI ethics, industry capstone project.

BITS Pilani vs IIT certificate

Choose BITS WILP for a full M.Tech degree, longer commitment and global mobility. Choose an IIT certificate for faster completion (6 months), lower fee (₹1.5L) and specific technical credentialling. Both available with enrollment support. Compare at datasciencecourse.in/compare.
