GenAI and LLM Courses India 2026 — Best Certificates for Working Professionals

Why GenAI certification matters in 2026

By 2026, Generative AI has moved from an experimental technology to a core business tool across BFSI, healthcare, retail, manufacturing and technology sectors. Professionals who can build, deploy and manage GenAI applications are in strong demand — and certifications from credible institutions signal genuine capability rather than surface-level familiarity.

IIT Madras GenAI & LLMs — the standout option

The Advanced Certificate Programme in ML, GenAI & LLMs by IITM Pravartak is the strongest GenAI-specific certification available through an IIT in 2026. It covers LLMs (Large Language Models), Transformers architecture, prompt engineering, fine-tuning, building AI applications with Python, deploying GenAI solutions and business use cases. The program is from NIRF #1 institution — IIT Madras — making the credential strong for job applications and promotion conversations.

Who should take the IIT Madras GenAI program

Software engineers and developers wanting to build GenAI applications. Data scientists wanting to add GenAI skills to their ML portfolio. IT professionals in organisations adopting AI who want a credible qualification. Budget: ₹1.5L + GST — justified for the IIT Madras credential.
