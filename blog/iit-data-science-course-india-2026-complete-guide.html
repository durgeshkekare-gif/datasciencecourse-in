IIT Madras BSc Data Science — India's best online DS degree

The IIT Madras Online BSc Data Science and Applications is unique in India: it's an actual BSc degree from India's #1 ranked engineering institution that you can earn entirely online without JEE. Structure: 4-year (can exit with certificate/diploma after 1 or 2 years). Entry: Class 12 pass + IIT Madras own qualifier exam (held twice a year). Cost: ~₹2–3L total. This is the best route for Class 12 students or graduates who want IIT Madras credentials in data science without clearing JEE.

E&ICT Academy — what it is and why all IIT cert fees are the same

The E&ICT (Electronics & ICT) Academy is a consortium of 9 IITs funded by MeitY (Ministry of Electronics and IT), Govt of India. This consortium standardises both curriculum and pricing across member IITs — which is why IIT Roorkee, IIT Guwahati, IIT Hyderabad, IIT BHU, and IIT Indore all charge ~₹1.5L for 6-month data science certificates. The lead institutes are IIT Guwahati, IIT Roorkee, and IIT Patna. Faculty are actual IIT professors, and each certificate carries the respective IIT's name.
