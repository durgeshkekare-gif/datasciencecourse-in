IIM Review · March 2026

IIM Kozhikode Applied Analytics & GenAI Programme 2026 — Is It Worth It?

What makes IIM Kozhikode's analytics program different

The Professional Certificate Programme in Applied Analytics & GenAI for Business Decisions from IIM Kozhikode is the only IIM program in this list that requires zero coding background. It is designed for business professionals — managers, MBAs, consultants, finance and marketing leaders — who want to use data analytics and GenAI as strategic tools without becoming data engineers.

Who it is for

Business managers, MBAs, finance and consulting professionals who need to understand data-driven decision-making. Professionals who manage data science teams and want to communicate more effectively with technical colleagues. Senior managers who want to lead AI initiatives without building models themselves.

What it covers

Descriptive, predictive and prescriptive analytics. Machine learning concepts (conceptual, not hands-on coding). Generative AI for business applications — using LLMs, building AI-powered workflows, evaluating AI output. Data storytelling and visualisation. Business case studies using analytics.

IIM Kozhikode credential

IIM Kozhikode is NIRF #10 in management and AACSB accredited. The PCP credential from IIMK carries strong weight in management circles. The program does not provide IIMK alumni status — it is a certificate program, not a degree.

Fee and duration

Fee: ~₹2L + GST. Duration: 1 year (weekend live sessions). For business professionals who cannot code — this is the strongest analytics credential available from an IIM at this price point.
